			V. Often asked questions
xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Here are few tips of how things work around the Password Generator:

Question 1. How can I generate password successfully? 

	Check all password properties you want and click "Generate password"-button.

Question 2. How to check password's strength? 

	After generating password succesfully, press "Copy Password"-Button on the left side of the main window and then click
	the "Check Password Strength Here"-link. At the website paste the just copied password to wanted text bar. 
	The website application will tell you how long it will take to crack your password.

Question 3. How to send password to e-mail?
	
	 After generating password to e-mail, click "Send password to e-mail"-button. 
         Here type your e-mail address to the wanted tex box, click "Save" and then press "Send Password"-button. 
	 Now check your e-mail! Here is the sender's e-mail address: pwgenerator93@gmail.com ;)

Question 4. How to encrypt a password? 

	After sending password successfully to e-mail of your choice, press the "Encryption"-button, check your e-mail for 
	the crypted password that was send to you and copy the e-mails encrypted password here. The application will now show you 
	the real password as a plain text.
